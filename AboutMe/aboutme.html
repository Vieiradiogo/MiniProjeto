"To me, photography is an art of observation. It’s about finding something interesting in an ordinary place… I’ve found it has little to do with the things you see and everything to do with the way you see them. Photography is an austere and blazing poetry of the real." - Elliott Erwitt

Welcome! I'm Francisco Vicente, a 23-year-old residing in the vibrant city of Leiria, Portugal. Photography isn't just a hobby; it's my heartbeat, my muse, and the canvas upon which I paint with light.

While I'm on the path to making photography my full-time gig, I've already delved into an eclectic array of projects. From capturing the pulsating energy of nightclubs to sculpting the perfect frame for models, cars, and bikes, my portfolio spans a diverse spectrum of subjects. Each click of the shutter is an opportunity to weave stories, evoke emotions, and freeze moments in time.

Fuelled by an unquenchable thirst for exploration, I thrive on immersing myself in different environments and cultures. Whether I'm traversing cobblestone streets or tearing through rugged mountain trails on my trusty downhill mountain bike, I find inspiration in every corner of this magnificent world.

Through my lens, I strive to unveil the beauty and uniqueness inherent in every subject. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for storytelling, I aim to craft images that not only captivate the eye but also ignite the imagination.

Join me on this visual odyssey as I continue to push boundaries, chase dreams, and share my love for photography with the world. Together, let's capture moments that transcend time and leave a lasting impression.
